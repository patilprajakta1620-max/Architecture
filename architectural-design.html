What we do — Architectural Design

We develop a clear architectural strategy that addresses site context, climate, client lifestyle and budget. Our deliverables include concept sketches, floor plans, elevations, 3D visualizations and full working drawings for approvals and construction.

Core Steps
  1. Discovery & Brief: Understand site, user needs and budget.
  2. Concept Design: Multiple schematic options with quick massing models.
  3. Design Development: Refined layouts, materials and structural coordination.
  4. Documentation: Working drawings, BOQ and approval packs.
  5. Construction Support: Site visits, clarifications and QA.
Deliverables
  • Concept sketches & mood boards
  • 2D floor plans & elevations
  • 3D renders & walkthroughs (optional)
  • Working drawings & BOQ
